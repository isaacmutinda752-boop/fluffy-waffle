About Webrayz

Your trusted partner in navigating the beauty of Kenya. Safe, Reliable, and Memorable.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for travel and a deep love for Kenya, Webrayz began as a small transport service dedicated to showing visitors the true beauty of our country. Over the years, we have grown into a premier transport partner for tourists and locals alike.

We believe that the journey is just as important as the destination. That's why we prioritize well-maintained vehicles, professional drivers, and personalized itineraries to ensure your Kenyan adventure is seamless from start to finish.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and memorable transport experiences that connect travelers to Kenya's natural wonders and cultural heritage.

Our Vision

To be the leading transport choice in East Africa, known for excellence in service, sustainability, and promoting local tourism.

Our Values

Safety first, Customer Satisfaction, Integrity, and Respect for Nature and Local Communities.
