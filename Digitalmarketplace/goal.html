Empowering Kenya's Digital Economy

At SokoDigital, we are bridging the gap between talent and opportunity. Our goal is to create a seamless ecosystem where Kenyan creators can monetize their skills and businesses can access world-class digital assets instantly.

Our Mission

To democratize access to digital commerce for every Kenyan creator, providing a secure and efficient platform to sell software, templates, and courses to a local and global audience.

Our Vision

To be the leading digital marketplace in East Africa, fostering a community of innovation where digital products drive business growth and personal prosperity.

Our Core Values

Trust: Secure M-Pesa payments.
Speed: Instant product delivery.
Quality: Curated high-standard assets.
